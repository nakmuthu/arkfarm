Areca Palm

Dypsis lutescens

Common Names: Areca Palm, Butterfly Palm, Golden Cane Palm, Yellow Palm

Local Name: Areca Panai (Tamil), Sona Khajur (Hindi)

Family: Arecaceae

Origin: Madagascar

Plant Type: Perennial clumping palm

Average Lifespan: 10–20 years in cultivation

🌿 Botanical Description
Growth HabitMulti-stemmed clumping palm; graceful arching fronds
Plant Size6–12 meters tall; clump spread 3–5 meters
LeavesPinnate fronds 2–3 meters long; bright green with yellow-green petioles
FlowersSmall, yellow; in branched inflorescences below the crown
FruitSmall oval drupe, 1–2 cm; yellow to orange when ripe
Root SystemFibrous, shallow; sensitive to root disturbance
☀ Growing Conditions
ClimateTropical to subtropical; humid preferred
Temperature Range16–35°C; frost-sensitive
Soil TypeWell-drained, fertile loamy soil; avoid waterlogging
Soil pH Preference6.0–7.0
Sun RequirementBright indirect light to full sun; tolerates partial shade
Water RequirementModerate; keep moist but not waterlogged
Can it Grow in Pots?Yes; popular indoor plant in large containers
🌾 Cultivation & Care
Fertilizer ScheduleMonthly with palm-specific fertilizer; magnesium and potassium important
Common PestsSpider mites, scale insects, mealybugs
Common DiseasesPink rot, leaf spot, root rot from overwatering
🌱 Pollination & Propagation
Propagation by SeedsFresh seeds germinate in 2–6 months; soak 24 hours before sowing
Propagation by CuttingsClump division; separate offshoots with roots
💧 Environmental Impact & Sustainability
Water UsageModerate; efficient with drip irrigation
Biodiversity BenefitsProvides shade and microhabitat; excellent air purifier indoors
